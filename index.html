Nebraska Addiction and Mental Health Treatment Directory

Nebraska Treatment Connect is your comprehensive resource for finding quality addiction and mental health treatment facilities across the state of Nebraska. Our directory includes residential treatment centers, halfway houses, outpatient services, and detox centers serving communities from Omaha to Lincoln, Grand Island to Norfolk, and everywhere in between.

Treatment Services Available in Nebraska

Residential Treatment

24/7 supervised care in a structured environment for individuals requiring intensive addiction or mental health treatment.

Halfway Houses

Transitional living facilities providing support and structure for individuals in recovery returning to independent living.

Outpatient Services

Flexible treatment programs allowing individuals to maintain work and family commitments while receiving care.

Detox Centers

Medically supervised withdrawal management services to safely begin the recovery process.

Getting Help in Nebraska

If you or a loved one is struggling with addiction or mental health issues, help is available. Nebraska offers a wide range of treatment options, from intensive residential programs to flexible outpatient services. Our directory makes it easy to find licensed, professional treatment facilities that meet your specific needs.

Crisis Resources: If you're experiencing a mental health crisis, call 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline) or go to your nearest emergency room. For substance abuse emergencies, contact your local emergency services at 911.
